Hey! Doggodex was wrong!

Well, its not always correct. Doggodex achieved around an 80% accuracy during training and was trained to only classify 120 different breeds of dogs. There are approximately 300+ breeds of dogs according to online sources, and Doggodex tries to give you a single result, so it will try its best to give you a definitive answer according to the model. The scenarios Doggodex struggles with are:

  • detecting a dog of mixed breeds
  • dog breeds outside of the training dataset,
  • images that contain a lot of visual noise
  • images that do not contain a dog
  • identifying the breed of a dog that shares a lot of visual similarties to other dog breeds
