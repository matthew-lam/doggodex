Hey! Doggodex was wrong!

Well, its not always correct. Doggodex achieved around an 80% accuracy during training and was trained to only classify 120 different breeds of dogs. There are approximately 300+ breeds of dogs according to online sources, and Doggodex tries to give you a single result, so it doesn't account for problems such as:

  • detecting a dog of mixed breeds
  • dog breeds outside of the training dataset,
  • images that contain a lot of visual noise or not of a dog
  • identifying the exact breed of a dog, especially if it looks very similar to another breed
    • and so on.
